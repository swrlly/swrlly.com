  2. Using the three step process, find the six trigonmetric functions of $t = \frac{7\pi}{6}$.
  3. Using the three step process, Find the six trigonmetric functions of $t = -\frac{2\pi}{3}$.
  4. Why is $\tan\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)$ undefined?
  5. $P\left(-\dfrac{3}{5}, -\dfrac{4}{5}\right)$ is a terminal point associated with some $t \in \mathbb{R}$. Find $\sin(t), \cos(t)$ and $\tan(t)$.

  7. Let's say $\cos(t) = -\frac{7}{25}$ and the terminal point of $t$ is in Quadrant III. What is $\sin(t)$ and $\tan(t)$?
  8. State each relevant transformation, amplitude, and period. Graph one complete period by hand. Be sure to show in the graph the endpoints of one period.
    1. $y = 1 + \cos \pi x$
    2. $y = \dfrac{1}{2} - \dfrac{1}{2}\cos\left(2x - \dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)$

    6. $y = 4 \tan (3x - 2\pi)$
